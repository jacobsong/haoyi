About Us

Haoyi Hardware Company® is a worldwide market retailer that was established in 1988. Over thirty years of being in the hardware business with local and foreign clients, we understand your needs: quality control, on-time delivery, and efficient communication. We are here to fulfill your inquiries, please contact us via email or telephone and check out our latest product catalogue.
